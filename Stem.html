STEM

STEM is an acronym for Science, Technology, Engineering and Math. Now days, these four academic disciplines go together. Since new projects are being invented and innovated, all four of the already mentioned are working together to create a better future. One example would be when an engineer and a technologicalist came up with the smartphones, then people knowledgeable of science had to see if it would not damage humans when using it. Also a mathematician had to work on the most profitable material to make the smartphone out of and the way to marketize it. In conclusion, STEM is not just four different fields in the business world, but a great team to build a better future.
