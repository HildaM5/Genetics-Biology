GMO in Food

Genetically modified organisms (GMOs) can be defined as organisms (plants, animals or microorganisms) in which the genetic material (DNA) has been altered in a way that does not occur naturally by mating and /or natural recombination. This technology is often called “modern biotechnology,” “gene technology” or “genetic engineering.” However, this isn’t new to some people. GMO in food had been used by some farmers to grow different kind of crops and also they can modify how it grows and even when it grows. Some sources of Genetically Modified Organisms are found in meat, eggs, and dairy from animals that have eaten GM feed. In my opinion, this is an amazing tool to use it to make better food, but as amazing it is as dangerous it could end up being to humans.
